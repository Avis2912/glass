Testing Your Native macOS App

If your Glass Notification app is running, select any text below to see the beautiful glass notification appear!

Sample Text for Selection

This is a sample paragraph of text that you can select to test the Glass Notification app. Simply click and drag to select any portion of this text, and you should see a beautiful translucent notification appear in the bottom-right corner of your screen.

The notification will display a preview of the selected text and automatically disappear after 3 seconds. Try selecting different lengths of text to see how the notification handles truncation for longer selections.

Technical Details

The Glass Notification app uses:

  • macOS Accessibility APIs to detect text selection system-wide
  • SwiftUI for the beautiful glassmorphic interface
  • NSWindow with custom styling for the translucent effect
  • Smooth animations for fade-in and fade-out effects
